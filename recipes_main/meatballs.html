Chicken Meatballs


Description :

Craving for a delicious crispy meaty snack? Then here is a simple yet amazing meatball recipe, which anyone can make at home. Made with the goodness of boiled chicken, spices and herbs, these crispy homemade meatballs can make for a perfect binge worthy treat you can serve for your parties and get togethers. Just follow this simple recipe given below and enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 300 gm chicken
  • 4 green chilli
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika powder
  • water as required
  • black pepepr as required
  • 6 clove garlic
  • 2 handfuls coriander leaves
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon corn flour
  • salt as required
  • refined oil as required

Steps

  1. Boil the chicken To make this simple dish, wash, clean and pressure cook the chicken for 2-3 whistles. Once the chicken attains room temperature.
  2. Make a smooth mixture Take a grinder, add the chicken after shredding it using a knife. Make a mixture with green chilies, coriander leaves, lemon juice, ginger, garlic, paprika, salt and pepper. Make a smooth coarse mixture and mix it with corn flour.
  3. Fry and serve hot Make small meatballs. In the meantime, take a pan and add oil. Once the oil is hot enough gently slide the meatballs, shallow fry by tossing the sides. Serve hot with a dip or sauce and enjoy.
